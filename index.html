About

Designer focused on what matters — people and outcomes.

With over 8 years in the field, I've partnered with global companies like Walmart, FNZ, Banco do Brasil and Bradesco to design complex digital products that scale. My work sits at the intersection of user research, product strategy and visual craft.

I'm specialized in UX strategy and product discovery — helping teams ask the right questions before jumping to solutions. I've built design systems from the ground up, led cross-functional squads, and shipped products used by millions.

I believe great design is invisible: when things work so naturally that users don't think twice, that's when I know the job is done.
